Welcome

Welcome to the UVa Women's Club and to the the many varied activities and interest groups you'll find coming up throughout the year.


Purpose

The purpose of the University of Virginia Women's Club is to be of service to the UVa community, to support philanthropy, and to promote sociability among its members. Membership is open to any woman who is or has been employed by UVa or who has a spouse/partner who is or has been employed by UVa. We offer a variety of interest groups: book club, gardening, bridge, crafting, hiking, gourmet food, and antiques. We enjoy various trips and theater excursions, as well as outstanding speakers at our fall and spring meetings.
